Starting Out

My first computer science class was in 8th grade where we learned HTML and CSS, but my journey in Computer Science began before then. Due to how much I loved playing on the computer with my father, I built a computer at the age of thirteen. Still to this day I recall the fond memories of putting the computer parts together. After that, my interests in computer science and other related fields skyrocketed.

Education
